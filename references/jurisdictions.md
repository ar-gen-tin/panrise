# Jurisdiction Quick Guide for Solo Founders

## Tier 1: Best for Most Solo Founders

### 🇺🇸 United States (Wyoming / Delaware LLC)
- **Solo score**: ★★★★★
- **Corp tax**: 0% federal (pass-through for non-US owners)
- **Personal tax**: 0% for non-US owners; US citizens pay federal income + SE tax
- **Setup**: $100-500 | Timeline: 1-3 days
- **Annual cost**: $60-600
- **Banking**: Mercury (remote), Wise
- **Good for**: Global SaaS, digital services, any solo founder wanting US credibility
- **Watch out**: US citizens face SE tax (~15.3%); EIN required for banking
- **Substance**: None required for non-US LLC owners

### 🇸🇬 Singapore
- **Solo score**: ★★★★
- **Corp tax**: 17% (effective ~8.5% first SGD 200K)
- **Capital gains**: 0%
- **Dividend WHT**: 0%
- **Setup**: SGD 300-800 | Timeline: 1-2 days
- **Annual cost**: SGD 3,000-5,000 (nominee director + secretary + filing)
- **Banking**: DBS, OCBC (may need to visit)
- **Good for**: Asia-focused businesses, revenue >$50K
- **Watch out**: Ongoing costs make it expensive for small operations; need resident director

### 🇪🇪 Estonia (e-Residency OÜ)
- **Solo score**: ★★★★
- **Corp tax**: 0% retained / 20% on distributions
- **Setup**: €385 total | Timeline: 2-4 weeks
- **Annual cost**: €500-1,500
- **Banking**: Wise Business (primary), LHV
- **Good for**: EU market access, profit reinvestment, fully remote admin
- **Watch out**: Not a tax residency; must pay personal tax where you actually live

### 🇦🇪 Dubai (Freezone)
- **Solo score**: ★★★★ (if you'll live there)
- **Corp tax**: 9% (>AED 375K) / 0% below
- **Personal tax**: 0%
- **Setup**: AED 5,000-12,000 | Timeline: 3-10 days
- **Annual cost**: AED 10,000-20,000 (license + visa + office + insurance)
- **Banking**: Emirates NBD, Mashreq
- **Good for**: Founders wanting 0% personal tax AND a base
- **Watch out**: Must actually live there; high cost of living; banking can be slow

## Tier 2: Good for Specific Situations

### 🇬🇧 United Kingdom
- **Solo score**: ★★★
- **Corp tax**: 19% (first £50K), 25% (above £250K)
- **Setup**: £12 | Timeline: Same day
- **Annual cost**: £200-500
- **Banking**: Wise, Revolut (high street banks difficult for non-residents)
- **Good for**: UK clients, cheapest incorporation globally
- **Watch out**: 19% tax is higher than US LLC (0%) or Estonia (0% retained)

### 🇭🇰 Hong Kong
- **Solo score**: ★★★
- **Corp tax**: 8.25% (first HKD 2M), 16.5% above
- **Offshore income**: 0% (if properly structured)
- **Dividend WHT**: 0%
- **Setup**: HKD 3,000-5,000 | Timeline: 1-2 weeks
- **Annual cost**: HKD 5,000-10,000
- **Good for**: China/Asia trade, offshore income structures
- **Watch out**: FSIE rules (2023+) tightened; substance increasingly required; political uncertainty

### 🇨🇦 Canada
- **Solo score**: ★★★
- **Corp tax**: 12-19% (small business) combined federal + provincial
- **Setup**: CAD 200-500 | Timeline: 1-5 days
- **Annual cost**: CAD 1,000-2,000
- **Good for**: North American market, R&D (SR&ED 15-35% credit), AI/tech talent
- **Watch out**: Higher taxes than US LLC; mandatory 25% Canadian-resident directors (federal corp)

### 🇦🇺 Australia
- **Solo score**: ★★
- **Corp tax**: 25% (small business, turnover <AUD 50M)
- **Setup**: AUD 500-1,000 | Timeline: 1-3 days
- **Annual cost**: AUD 1,000-3,000
- **Good for**: R&D intensive (43.5% tax credit), Australian market
- **Watch out**: 25% corporate tax is high; superannuation (11.5%) adds to cost

## Tier 3: Special Purpose / Home Market Only

### 🇯🇵 Japan
- **Solo score**: ★★
- **Corp tax**: ~30% combined
- **Good for**: Only if your customers are Japanese or you live in Japan
- **Watch out**: High tax, complex compliance, language barrier

### 🇰🇷 South Korea
- **Solo score**: ★★
- **Corp tax**: 9-24% (tiered)
- **Good for**: Korean market, K-content industry
- **Watch out**: Small domestic market, language barrier

### 🇨🇳 China (WFOE)
- **Solo score**: ★
- **Corp tax**: 25% (15% high-tech)
- **Good for**: Only if you MUST operate in China mainland
- **Watch out**: ODI/SAFE for outbound; complex registration (2-4 months); capital controls

### 🇳🇿 New Zealand
- **Solo score**: ★★
- **Corp tax**: 28%
- **Good for**: Paired with Australia, agritech, clean brand
- **Watch out**: 28% flat rate, small market
