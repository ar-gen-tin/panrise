## Breakdown: What You Actually Pay

### Wyoming LLC
- State filing: $100
- Registered agent: $50-100/year
- Annual report: $60
- EIN application: Free
- Mercury bank: Free
- Wise Business: Free (pay per transfer)
- Accounting: $500 (simple) to $1,500 (with US tax return prep)

### Delaware LLC
- State filing: $90
- Franchise tax: $300/year minimum
- Registered agent: $100-200/year
- EIN: Free
- Mercury bank: Free
- Accounting: Similar to Wyoming

### Singapore Pte. Ltd.
- ACRA registration: SGD 315
- Nominee director: SGD 2,000-3,500/year
- Company secretary: SGD 300-600/year
- Registered address: SGD 300-600/year
- Annual return filing: SGD 60
- Corporate tax filing: SGD 500-1,000 (accountant)
- Audit: Exempt if revenue <SGD 10M and <50 employees

### Estonia OÜ
- e-Residency card: €120
- Company registration: €265
- Contact person: €100-200/year
- Annual report: Included in accounting
- Accounting: €50-100/month (Estonian service providers)
- Distribution tax: 20% only when you take money out

### Dubai Freezone
- License: AED 5,000-12,000/year (varies by freezone)
- Visa: AED 3,000-5,000 (first year)
- Emirates ID + medical: AED 1,000-2,000
- Virtual office: AED 5,000-10,000/year
- Health insurance: AED 3,000-8,000/year (mandatory)
- Accounting: AED 3,000-7,000/year

## Hidden Costs to Watch

1. **Currency conversion**: 0.4-3% on every transfer (Wise: 0.4%, PayPal: 3%)
2. **Payment processing**: 2.9% + $0.30 per Stripe transaction
3. **VAT compliance**: $2,000-5,000/year if you self-file (or 5-10% via MoR)
4. **Tax return preparation**: $300-1,500 depending on complexity
5. **Registered agent renewal**: $50-200/year (easy to forget)
6. **Bank account maintenance**: Some banks charge if balance < minimum

## Revenue Breakpoints: When to Level Up

| Revenue | Recommended Structure | Monthly Overhead |
|---------|----------------------|-----------------|
| $0-5K | No company (personal invoicing) | $0 |
| $5K-25K | Wyoming LLC or UK Ltd | $50-100 |
| $25K-100K | Same + proper accounting | $100-250 |
| $100K-500K | Consider Singapore/Estonia for tax optimization | $300-700 |
| $500K+ | Multi-jurisdiction structure + accountant + legal counsel | $1,000+ |

## ROI Calculation: Is It Worth Incorporating?

### Example: Solo SaaS founder, $80K/year revenue, living in Germany (42% marginal tax)

**Option A: German Freelancer (Freiberufler)**
- Income tax: ~$28,000 (35% effective)
- Health insurance: ~$6,000
- Total tax burden: ~$34,000
- Admin cost: ~$1,000
- **Net after tax: ~$45,000**

**Option B: Estonia OÜ + German tax residency**
- Estonian corporate tax: $0 (retained profits)
- German personal tax on salary: Depends on salary level drawn
- If draw €40K salary: ~€10K German tax + €6K health
- Retain €40K in OÜ at 0%
- Admin cost: ~$2,500
- **Net position: Better by ~$10-15K** (deferred tax on retained)
- **BUT**: Must actually be managing company from Germany; substance risk if CFC

**Bottom line**: Optimization only makes meaningful difference above ~$50K-75K annual profit. Below that, keep it simple.
