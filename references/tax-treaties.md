# Key Tax Treaties for Solo Founders

## Why DTAs Matter for You

A Double Tax Agreement (DTA) prevents you from paying tax twice on the same income. As a solo founder, DTAs matter when:
- Your company is in Country A and you live in Country B
- You receive dividends, royalties, or service fees across borders
- A client withholds tax from your payments

## The 5 Routes That Matter Most

### Route 1: US LLC → Anywhere (Pass-Through)
- **How it works**: US LLC doesn't pay US tax (pass-through to owner)
- **DTA relevance**: LOW — no corporate-level tax event
- **Your tax**: Only where YOU are personally tax-resident
- **Key benefit**: No withholding on most payments to LLC

### Route 2: Singapore → You (Dividends)
- **Singapore dividend WHT**: 0% (Singapore never withholds on dividends)
- **No DTA needed** for dividend extraction
- **Your personal tax**: Depends on your tax residency country
- **Key benefit**: Cleanest profit extraction in Asia

### Route 3: Estonia → EU (Retained Profits)
- **Estonia corporate tax**: 0% while retained
- **Distribution tax**: 20% when profits paid out
- **Within EU**: No additional withholding (Parent-Subsidiary Directive)
- **To non-EU**: Check DTA (most reduce to 5-10%)

### Route 4: Hong Kong → Anywhere
- **HK dividend WHT**: 0%
- **Offshore profits**: 0% HK tax
- **Combined**: If income is genuinely offshore, effective tax near 0%
- **Watch**: FSIE rules (2023+) require substance for passive income exemption

### Route 5: Dubai → Anywhere
- **UAE dividend WHT**: 0%
- **UAE personal tax**: 0%
- **Corporate tax**: 9% on >AED 375K
- **DTA network**: 130+ treaties
- **Key benefit**: Low corporate + zero personal = lowest legal combined rate

## Common DTA Rates (Dividend Withholding)

| From → To | Rate | Notes |
|-----------|------|-------|
| Singapore → any | 0% | Singapore never withholds |
| Hong Kong → any | 0% | HK never withholds |
| Estonia → EU | 0% | EU directive |
| Estonia → non-EU | 7-15% | Check specific DTA |
| US LLC → owner | N/A | Pass-through, no WHT event |
| UK → any | 0% | UK doesn't withhold on dividends |
| Dubai/UAE → any | 0% | No WHT |

## For US Citizens Specifically

US DTAs don't help you avoid US tax (you owe US tax on worldwide income regardless). But they help with:
- **Foreign Tax Credit (FTC)**: Credit foreign taxes paid against US liability
- **Avoiding double taxation**: DTA ensures you don't pay full tax in both countries
- **Treaty tie-breaker**: If two countries both claim you as tax resident, DTA determines which one wins

## When DTAs DON'T Help

1. **Pass-through entities**: US LLC income isn't subject to DTA WHT rates
2. **Tax havens with no DTAs**: Cayman, BVI have few/no DTAs
3. **Same-country transactions**: DTA only applies cross-border
4. **Below treaty thresholds**: Small payments often below minimum for withholding

## Bottom Line for Solo Founders

- **Using US LLC?** → DTAs barely matter (pass-through)
- **Using Singapore/HK?** → 0% WHT on dividends regardless of DTA
- **Using Estonia OÜ?** → EU distributions are DTA-free; non-EU check rates
- **Using Dubai?** → 0% everything, DTAs are bonus insurance
- **Living in high-tax country?** → DTA ensures you get credit for corporate tax paid
