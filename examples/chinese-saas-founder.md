# Example: Chinese SaaS Founder Going Global

**User prompt:** "I'm a Chinese mainland developer making $120K/year from a SaaS product. My customers are global. Where should I register my company?"

---

## Panrise Solo Response

### User Profile
`CN | SaaS | $120K | global | minimize_tax`

### Recommended Structure
**Hong Kong Limited Company (有限公司)**

### Why This Works for You
- Escapes China's $50K/year individual forex limit — HK has zero forex controls
- Stripe HK available for global payment collection
- Offshore income can qualify for 0% HK profits tax (if properly structured)
- 1-2 hours from Shenzhen — easy to manage

### Setup Steps
1. Choose company name (Chinese + English) — 1 day
2. Register with HK Companies Registry — 1-2 weeks
3. Appoint company secretary (HK resident required, ~HKD 1,000/year)
4. Open bank account (ZA Bank online, or HSBC with in-person visit) — 1-3 weeks
5. Connect Stripe HK — 3-5 days
6. Apply for Business Registration Certificate

### Banking & Payments
- **Primary:** ZA Bank (free, online opening) — BUT requires HKID for business accounts
- **Alternative:** Airwallex HK (no HKID needed, 60+ currencies)
- **Payments:** Stripe HK (3.4% + HKD 2.35 per transaction)
- **Multi-currency:** Wise Business for international transfers

### Tax Obligations
- HK Profits Tax: 8.25% on first HKD 2M, 16.5% above (offshore income may be exempt)
- China personal income tax: declare HK company income in annual 汇算清缴 (March 1 - June 30)
- Dividend tax: 20% when distributing from HK company to mainland individual
- CRS: HK bank accounts automatically reported to China tax bureau

### Estimated Costs
| Item | Year 1 | Ongoing |
|------|--------|---------|
| Company registration | HKD 3-5K ($400-650) | — |
| Company secretary | HKD 1K ($130) | HKD 1K/year |
| Annual return | HKD 105 | HKD 105/year |
| Accounting | HKD 5-10K ($650-1,300) | HKD 5-10K/year |
| **Total** | **~$1,300-2,100** | **~$900-1,500/year** |

### Watch Out For
- **CRS enforcement is real (2025+):** China tax bureau actively contacting people with unreported HK accounts
- **Offshore claim scrutiny:** IRD increasingly challenging offshore exemptions — keep board meetings outside HK
- **Don't use USDT to move money:** Criminal penalties start at RMB 200K
- **Form your HK company in your personal name** (not through a mainland entity) to avoid ODI filing complexity

*This is general guidance. For your specific situation, consult a qualified tax advisor.*
